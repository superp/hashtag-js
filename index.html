External hashtags demo

October 07 2014
external hashtags, demo

Click any hashtag you find in the post to see how external hashtags really work. External hashtags can be used as an additional source of information about the topic.

So, the next time you are going to write something in your blog about your new Nikes, just add hashtag #nike to the post.

So, the next time you have a cool ride with #uber, share your experience and don't just mention Uber's Facebook page, but post a hashtag also.

So, the next time you have an exciting vacation living in a cozy apartment rent through #airbnb, and you decide to write your new post about it, add a hashtag.

External hashtags provide users with content gathered across different social media networks in one beautiful feed. You can customize settings of the feed through your personal admin panel.
